Dr. Samuel Hayes

Strategic Designer by ministry, military, business, and academic leaders

Dr. Samuel Hayes, Jr. is distinguished as a strategic designer by ministry, military, business, and academic leaders.

Lieutenant Colonel Hayes (retired) has decades of experience includes assignments in 12 different countries across three continents as a special operations officer in the U.S. Army.
During the same time, he thrived as a businessman, scholar, and minister, which prepared him to develop the next generation of Kingdom strategists.
Apostle Sam is the International Director of International Coalition of Apostolic Leaders (ICAL). He is also the Founder and President of Samuel Hayes Ministries, Inc. and leads a marriage ministry with his wife, Dr. Andrea Hayes. 

Sam is an international best-selling author, and his books distill insights on relational leadership, global networks, design thinking, marriage, and strategy. 
Dr. Hayes and his family reside in North Carolina.
