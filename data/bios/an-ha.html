Dr. Andrea Hayes

Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor (LCMHC), Author, and founder of Ladies of Vivid Elegance (L.O.V.E)

Dr. Andrea Hayes (Dr. Drea) is a Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor (LCMHC), an author, and founder of Ladies of Vivid Elegance (L.O.V.E) a mentorship program for young women. She also maintains a private practice in Fayetteville, North Carolina. Dr. Drea is a woman of God, proud military spouse, mother of three beautiful adult children, and former educator. The love she has for God motivates her to strive to help others grow and develop into who God has called them to be. Her passion for seeing others become successful and reach their fullest potential is the driving force behind her desire to continue to advance in the mental health field and in the Kingdom by continuing to gain knowledge while giving back to others.
