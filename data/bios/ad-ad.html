Apostle Michael Adeyemi Adefarasin

Chief Servant over Kingdom Lifeline Apostolic Ministries International

MICHAEL ADEYEMI ADEFARASIN    With a mandate from Isaiah 42:6-7. ‘Apostle Yemi’ as he is friendly called has a mission to raise leaders to influence society to tip the scales from the secular into kingdom culture.

An apostle with a strong prophetic mantle and strategic-level spiritual warfare characterized by demanding of territorial strongholds for the liberation of peoples.

With a call to nations, an international conference speaker and chief servant over Kingdom Lifeline Apostolic Ministries International (KLAM), he provides oversight and covering to numerous Ministries and Sons around the globe.

He is an International Conference speaker, He is married to Sharon and domiciled in Abuja Nigeria.
