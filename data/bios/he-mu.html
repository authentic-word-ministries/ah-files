Pastor Henry MUGISHA

Founder and Lead Pastor Community Life Church | Uganda, Kampala

He is the CEO of The DogMan,a company that imports, exports, breeds and trains dogs to offer domestic Security.

He holds a Masters of Arts in Global leadership from Fuller Theological Seminary, USA where he is currently a PhD candidate.

He is an adventure biker rider with Uganda Bikers who ride to support Cancer and HIV patients in Uganda.

Pastor Henry is married to Pamela Mugisha and they have been married for 19 years. God has blessed them with 3 children; Shayna, Shaine and Shawn.
