Pastor Tonya Hill

Founder of Abundant Life Institute | Published Author | Life Coach

Pastor Tonya Hill is a Published Author, Life Coach, International Speaker, and Founding Executive Director of Abundant Life Institute. A dedicated member of First Baptist Church North Indianapolis, she serves as the Director of Community Impact, Mental Health Initiative Director, and Cyber Pastor.

Pastor Tonya's divine calling is to help people understand their identity in Christ, facilitate healing, and support them in living the Abundant Life Christ desires for them to have. God has allowed her ministry to span Mexico, Jamaica, Tanzania, South Africa, Kenya, and the United States of America, focusing on encouraging, educating, and equipping God’s people.

With over 30 years of ministry experience, Pastor Tonya has organized and led impactful events like Citywide Revivals, Grooming of Queen Retreats, Man Up Conferences, CommU.N.I.T.Y Fairs, Anti-Gun Violence Conferences, Domestic Violence Conferences, Young Mother’s Outreach Projects, Leadership Conferences and Prayer Concerts.
 
The Abundant Life Institute, founded by Pastor Tonya, aims to end childhood sex abuse and help men, women, and children heal from trauma. Its mission is to create a world where no one is sexually abused (#NotOneMore). She also opened Tamar’s Sanctuary, which provides affordable housing, life coaching, and community connections to help women heal from sexual abuse and related trauma.

Pastor Tonya holds a degree in Sociology, a Bachelor’s in Social Work from Indiana University School of Social Work, a Master of Arts in Ministerial Leadership from Indiana Wesleyan Seminary, and a license of ordination from New Wineskin Ministries International.

Pastor Tonya’s first ministry is her family. She has been married to Mr. Gregory Hill for 39 years and loves being a wife, mother, and grandmother.
