Obii Pax- Harry

Apostolic and Prophetic leader and International conference speaker

Obii Pax-Harry is an apostolic and prophetic leader and international conference speaker recognized for her cutting-edge ministry across three continents (Europe, Africa and USA) for over three decades. She is also a serial entrepreneur with established track record in successful business enterprises.

Her unique development work across sectors of society has been recognized as pioneering, and catalytic with impact. Obii's leadership work focuses on mentoring generations of values centered, ethical leaders for nation building.  Her direct and indirect mentees continue to make visible contributions in their spheres of influence as industry leaders.

Obii is also a social change advocate, practitioner and coach to many transformational leaders who are driving change initiatives in their industry.
 
She is an acclaimed strategist and thought leader who has been a speaker at several institutions of higher learning including the student led African Development Conference at Kennedy School Of Government, Harvard University, Liberty University and Wagner University in the USA.
She was an executive member of the Christian Broadcasting Council of Great Britain (CBC).
Obii is an ordained minister and a church planter, who has pioneered several initiatives across three continents. She sits as a respected member on the council of several global leadership networks.
 
She is a trained and qualified lawyer. Obii is happily married.
