Pastor Felin Gakwaya

Leader of Authentic Word Ministries in the UK & The vice president of the Apostolic Centres in Europe

Pastor Felin Gakwaya was born in Rwanda-Kigali on 31st December 1970. He is the senior Pastor in charge of ZTCC-London, the founder of ZTCC UK E-Church. He is also the leader of Authentic Word Ministries in the UK, the vice president of the Apostolic Centres in Europe. Pastor Felin is an author having published a book (Prayer to Intercession in 2019). He is gospel singer and has written many gospel songs, produced gospel albums.

Pastor Felin is a man of prayer, an exceptional Intercessor. He trained as a lawyer and currently work as a senior producer for the BBC. Married to a beautiful wife Pastor Dadali Kalinda and have four children.
