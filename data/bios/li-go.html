Apostle Linda Gobodo

Founder of Church Kingdom Ministries/ Vuka Africa Foundation

Apostle Linda Gobodo is the founder of Vuka Africa Foundation which heralds the message of Hope, Healing and Restoration, calling Africans in the continent, and diaspora to take responsibility to rebuild their nations.

She was a Pastor of His Kingdom Church Ministries for 10 years since 1997, before receiving a call to establish the House of Prayer in the nations to awake the Church.

Since then, she has been travelling across the continent and beyond preaching the Gospel of the Kingdom of God through, and declaring the message of hope, healing and restoration for the continent and the people of Africa. She is one of the leaders of Healing of the Nations of Africa (HHR), and founder of Arise, Watch and Pray for Africa.

In 2017 she was awarded an Honorary Doctorate in Divinity and the Order of Ministry of Stateswoman by CICA an International University & Seminary in New York, USA. Her passion is to raise and equip leaders in the area of their calling and gifting through discipleship. She is an author and song writer.
