Bishop Dr. Joseph Mattera

Founder
United States Coalition of Apostolic Leaders

Dr. Joseph Mattera is an internationally-known author, futurist, consultant, and scholar whose mission is to influence leaders who influence nations. He is the overseeing bishop of Christ Covenant Coalition, the founder of the United States Coalition of Apostolic Leaders and is known as a global networker for movement leaders. He has a DMin. from Baake University, THD from Antioch University, and was a PHD candidate. His articles are posted by numerous media outlets, and routinely ricochet across the net globally. Dr. Mattera is renowned for addressing current events through the lens of Scripture by applying biblical truths and offering cogent defenses to today's postmodern culture.
